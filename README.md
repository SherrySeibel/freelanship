#Freelanship

######This is the prototype for Freelanship, a service that connects students to project-based internships.

Freelanship was born during the 2014 Boston [Startup Weekend](http://startupweekend.org/)

With only 52 hours of brainstorming and technical development, the Freelanship prototype was demonstrated 
to a panel of judges where it was .2 points away from placing. 

###The domonstration

Several pages were included in the demo, including the landing page, sign in page, and search results.
